Who is the Youth Council Choir?

The origins of the Youth Council (YC) choir at Mary Immaculate Parish (MIP) trace back to the early 1980s...

Prominent early members included Bro. Joel Abellanosa and Rene Ferrer...

The evolution of YC took an artistic turn with the emergence of Apostles of Christ Thru Service (ACTS)...

Today, the Youth Council Choir remains a vital part of Mary Immaculate Parish...

Vision and Mission

Mission:

To glorify God and honor the Blessed Virgin Mary through the power of music...

Vision:

To serve as a guiding light of Marian spirituality within our parish...

Activities and Contributions

  • Liturgical Services: Chorister services for weddings, funeral masses...
  • Spiritual Formation: Monthly Word of Life reflections...
  • Parish Outreach: Participating in feeding programs...
  • Music Ministry: Weekly rehearsals, monthly vigil services...

Profound Achievements

  • Top 5 placement in the "Sound of Christmas 2015"...
  • 4th place in the Villar Foundation's chorale competition 2024...
  • And many more...

Future Goals

  1. Greater involvement in choral competitions.
  2. More chorale clinics to refine their musical excellence.
  3. Increased collaborations with choirs from other parishes.
  4. Enhanced musical and spiritual Catholic Christian formations from the Diocese of Parañaque (DOP).
  5. Expanded community service within the parish.

Upcoming Milestones

  • 40th Anniversary Celebration: Scheduled for May 2025...
  • 2025 Parish Fiesta Hermano/Hermana Mayor: YC will make history...

Additional Participation
